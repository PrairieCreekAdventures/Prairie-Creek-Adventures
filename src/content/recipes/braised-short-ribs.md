Nothing about this recipe is quick. And that is entirely the point.

## Ingredients

- 4 lbs beef short ribs, bone-in
- Salt and pepper
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, roughly chopped
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 bottle dry red wine
- 2 cups beef stock
- 4 sprigs thyme
- 2 bay leaves

## Method

Heat the oven to 325°F. Season the short ribs generously with salt and pepper, then sear them in olive oil in a heavy Dutch oven, turning until every side is deeply browned. Set aside.

Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for 8 minutes until softened. Stir in the garlic and tomato paste and cook one more minute.

Pour in the wine and let it come to a simmer. Scrape up every brown bit from the bottom. Reduce by about half.

Return the ribs to the pot. Add the stock, thyme, and bay. The liquid should come about two-thirds up the ribs. Cover and braise in the oven for 2½ to 3 hours, until the meat is falling off the bone.

Rest for 15 minutes. Skim the fat. Serve over soft polenta or mashed potatoes, with good crusty bread for the sauce.
